Meeting Summaries

While we would love to see all our members at our meetings, we know that everyone is busy! If you're interested in joining but you can't make our meeting days, you are still welcome to participate in our events. Just make sure you fill out the membership form and pay your dues to officially become a member. Happy serving!

The Kronicles

Circle K members can submit articles to The Kronicle, Georgia Circle K's publication by the district's bulletin editor. In an issue of The Kronicle, members will find news, events, member spotlights, club spotlights, and introductions to district board members.
