Atlanta Community Food Bank

Come help sort goods and food for distribution to families in need.​

Who

Alternating Tuesdays and Wednesday every week, Circkle K'ers volunteer on-location for the Atlanta Community Foodbank. This organization strives to provide nutritious food for those in need, and relies on a network of volunteers to realize this goal. ​

What

Volunteers will participate in sorting and packaging food to be distributed to families in need. Make sure to wear close-toed shoes! ​
